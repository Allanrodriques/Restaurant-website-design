About Us

MyMasala was established in 2016 as a small growing food stall.Today we have 5 outlets over Mumbai

due to our evergrowing number of customers. We specialize in Indian and Chinese cuisine. We give

priority to quality as well as quantity of food. We use fresh products only in our dishes. Cleanliness is

also one of our main focuses as we aim to make our restaurant a healthy and safe environment.

As a COVID precautionary measure we sanitize our kitchen every hour and every table is sanitized

after a customer leaves. Come visit us and try our daily special dishes. You wont regret!

Q: How do tastings work?

A: We reserve several weekdays and Sundays for private tastings depending upon your schedule. We think it's important you taste everything on the menu, so you can go into your event confident that the food is amazing and what you envisioned. We require at least one-week advance notice to schedule a tasting.
